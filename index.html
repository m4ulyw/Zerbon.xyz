Zerbon

Clean to Earn – Turning Environmental Action into a Game

What is Zerbon?

Zerbon is a mobile-first eco-gaming platform that gamifies environmental cleanup activities. Players complete clean-up missions, progress through gameplay, and earn rewards while creating real-world social and environmental impact.

How It Works

Play

Players clean virtual environments through casual mobile gameplay.

Progress

Earn XP, unlock new areas, and upgrade tools and abilities.

Earn

Rewards are earned through in-game items and future ecosystem utilities.

Impact

Gameplay connects to real-world environmental cleanup initiatives.

Why Zerbon

High Engagement

Daily quests, progression systems, and social gameplay.

Scalable Model

Built for mass adoption with clear monetization paths.

Real Impact

Measurable social and environmental outcomes.
